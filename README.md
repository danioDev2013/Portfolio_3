On my portfolio's hompepage you will find a resume, an introductory, an about me paragraph, featured projects, and a footer with social links.
 * Dowload my resume by clicking the button at the top.
 * Click the more button to view more projects.
 * Click the project picture or title to view the deployed site or click the github icon to view the repo.
 * Scroll down to connect with me.
 * You can email me if you have any questions or click the envelope to fill out a contact form.
 * When you fill out the contact form it will say success, just click back to get back to my portfolio. 
 * Click any of my social links and follow me!
